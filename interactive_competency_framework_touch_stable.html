Experiential Learning Competency Framework
Relational | Reflective | Responsive
This interactive guide is designed for experiential educators who are new to the framework. Start with the dial to explore the seven domains. Then move into the underpinning competencies. Finally, open any competency to view its full descriptor in plain, readable form.
How to use this tool: tap a coloured segment on the dial or a domain chip for layer 1, select View competencies for layer 2, and open any competency card for layer 3.
